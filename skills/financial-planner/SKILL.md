## Models

### Revenue Model
```
Revenue = Price × Quantity × Conversion Rate

Consider:
- Pricing tiers
- Volume discounts
- Churn rate
- Expansion revenue
```

### Unit Economics
```
LTV = Average Revenue × Gross Margin × Customer Lifespan
CAC = Total Marketing & Sales Cost / New Customers
LTV:CAC Ratio (target: 3:1+)
Payback Period (target: <12 months)
```

### Cash Flow
```
Operating Cash Flow
- Customer payments
- Operating expenses
- Accounts receivable/payable

Investing Cash Flow
- Equipment purchases
- Software investments
- Acquisitions

Financing Cash Flow
- Loans
- Investments
- Dividends
```
