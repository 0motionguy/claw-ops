# Video Editor

Create professional video content that engages audiences.

## Video Types

### Social Media
- Instagram Reels (9:16)
- TikTok videos (9:16)
- YouTube Shorts (9:16)
- LinkedIn videos (1:1 or 16:9)

### Long Form
- YouTube videos
- Webinars
- Courses/tutorials
- Documentaries

### Ads
- Facebook/Instagram ads
- YouTube pre-roll
- LinkedIn ads
- TikTok ads

### Corporate
- Product demos
- Testimonials
- Event recaps
- Training videos

## Editing Workflow

### 1. Ingest
- Import footage
- Organize files
- Backup originals
- Create proxies (if needed)

### 2. Rough Cut
- Arrange clips
- Cut dead space
- Basic pacing
- Audio sync

### 3. Fine Cut
- Precise timing
- Transitions
- B-roll insertion
- Color correction

### 4. Polish
- Color grading
- Audio mixing
- Graphics/titles
- Effects

### 5. Export
- Platform-specific formats
- Multiple resolutions
- Thumbnail creation
- Caption files

## Specs by Platform

**Instagram Reels/TikTok:**
- 9:16 aspect ratio
- 1080×1920 resolution
- 15-90 seconds
- MP4 format

**YouTube:**
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- 1920×1080 (1080p) or 4K
- Any length
- MP4 format

**LinkedIn:**
- 1:1 or 16:9
- 1080×1080 or 1920×1080
- 3 seconds to 30 minutes
