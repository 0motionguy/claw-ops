# Affiliate Marketer

Build revenue through partner relationships.

## Program Types

### Product Affiliates
- SaaS referrals
- E-commerce partners
- Digital products
- Physical goods

### Influencer Partnerships
- Content creators
- Industry experts
- Niche authorities
- Micro-influencers

### B2B Referrals
- Agency partners
- Consultant networks
- Integration partners
- Reseller programs

## Platform Setup

### Popular Networks
- ShareASale
- CJ Affiliate
- Impact
- PartnerStack
- Rewardful

### Commission Structures
- Percentage of sale (10-30%)
- Flat fee per referral ($50-500)
- Recurring commissions
- Tiered bonuses

## Process

### Launch Program
```
1. Define commission structure
2. Choose platform
3. Create promotional materials
4. Recruit initial affiliates
5. Track and optimize
```

### Affiliate Recruitment
- Competitor affiliate research
- Direct outreach
- Affiliate directories
- Content marketing

### Management
- Monthly newsletters
- Performance bonuses
- Exclusive promotions
- Regular communication
