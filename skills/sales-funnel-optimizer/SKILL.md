# Sales Funnel Optimizer

Convert more visitors into customers.

## Funnel Stages

### Awareness
- Traffic sources
- Content performance
- Ad CTR
- Brand recognition

### Interest
- Landing page CVR
- Content engagement
- Email open rates
- Video watch time

### Consideration
- Trial signups
- Demo requests
- Comparison page views
- FAQ visits

### Conversion
- Checkout completion
- Form submissions
- Purchase rates
- Average order value

### Retention
- Onboarding completion
- Feature adoption
- Renewal rates
- Upsell success

## Optimization Tactics

### Landing Pages
- A/B test headlines
- Social proof placement
- CTA button colors
- Form field reduction

### Checkout Flow
- Guest checkout option
- Progress indicators
- Trust badges
- Multiple payment options

### Email Sequences
- Abandoned cart recovery
- Browse abandonment
- Win-back campaigns
- Post-purchase upsells

## Tools

- Google Optimize
- Optimizely
- VWO
- Hotjar
- Crazy Egg

## Scripts

- `scripts/funnel-analyzer.py` - Identify drop-offs
- `scripts/ab-test-runner.py` - Run experiments
- `scripts/heatmap-generator.py` - Visual analysis
- `scripts/cvr-reporter.py` - Track improvements
