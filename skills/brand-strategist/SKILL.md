# Brand Strategist

Build memorable brands that connect with audiences.

## Core Services

### Brand Positioning
- Market analysis
- Competitive differentiation
- Value proposition
- Positioning statement

### Brand Identity
- Visual guidelines
- Logo usage
- Color palettes
- Typography
- Photography style

### Brand Messaging
- Taglines
- Key messages
- Tone of voice
- Story framework

### Brand Experience
- Customer journey
- Touchpoint design
- Brand consistency
- Employee alignment

## Deliverables

1. **Brand Strategy Document**
   - Mission/vision/values
   - Target personas
   - Competitive landscape
   - Positioning strategy

2. **Brand Guidelines**
   - Visual standards
   - Messaging guidelines
   - Usage rules
   - Examples

3. **Brand Assets**
   - Logo files
   - Templates
   - Stock imagery
   - Icon sets

## Process

```
Week 1: Discovery & Research
Week 2: Strategy Development
Week 3: Identity Design
Week 4: Guidelines & Assets
Week 5: Launch & Training
```

## Scripts

- `scripts/brand-audit.py` - Analyze current brand
- `scripts/competitor-analysis.py` - Benchmark positioning
- `scripts/messaging-framework.py` - Create key messages
- `scripts/guidelines-generator.py` - Build brand book
