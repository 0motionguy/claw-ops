# Influencer Outreach

Partner with creators to amplify your brand.

## Process

### 1. Research & Identify
- Audience alignment
- Engagement quality
- Content fit
- Brand safety check

### 2. Outreach
- Personalized pitches
- Value proposition
- Collaboration ideas
- Clear CTAs

### 3. Negotiation
- Rates & deliverables
- Usage rights
- Timeline
- Contract terms

### 4. Management
- Campaign coordination
- Content review
- Performance tracking
- Relationship nurturing

## Platforms

**Discovery:**
- Modash
- Heepsy
- AspireIQ
- Upfluence

**Micro-Influencers:**
- Instagram (10K-100K)
- TikTok (10K-100K)
- YouTube (10K-100K)
- Twitter/X (5K-50K)

## Outreach Template

```
Subject: Collaboration opportunity with [Brand]

Hi [Name],

I've been following your content on [platform] and love your work on [specific topic/post].

I'm [Your name] from [Brand]. We're [brief description] and I think your audience would resonate with what we're building.

Would you be interested in a collaboration? I'd love to discuss:
- [Specific idea 1]
- [Specific idea 2]
- [Compensation/arrangement]

Let me know if you're interested and we can set up a quick call!

Best,
[Name]
```
