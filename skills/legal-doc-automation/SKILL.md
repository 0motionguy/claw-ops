# Legal Doc Automation

Generate legal documents quickly and consistently.

## Document Types

### Contracts
- Service agreements
- Consulting contracts
- Employment contracts
- Vendor agreements

### Business Formation
- LLC operating agreements
- Partnership agreements
- Shareholder agreements
- Bylaws

### Intellectual Property
- NDAs (mutual & one-way)
- IP assignment
- Licensing agreements
- Work-for-hire

### Terms & Policies
- Terms of service
- Privacy policy
- Cookie policy
- Refund policy

## Key Clauses

### Service Agreement Essentials
- Scope of work
- Payment terms
- Timeline
- Deliverables
- Revisions
- Termination
- Liability

### NDA Essentials
- Definition of confidential info
- Obligations of receiving party
- Exclusions
- Term
- Return of information
- Remedies

## Templates

### Contract Template
```
[COMPANY] SERVICE AGREEMENT

This agreement is between [Company] ("Provider") and [Client] ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider will deliver: [Description]

2. PAYMENT
Client will pay: $[Amount] [terms]

3. TIMELINE
Project will be completed by: [Date]

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
[Ownership terms]

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
[Limitation clause]

6. TERMINATION
[Termination terms]

Signed: _________________ Date: _______
```
