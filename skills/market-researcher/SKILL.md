# Market Researcher

Make data-driven decisions with confidence.

## Research Types

### Customer Research
- Surveys & interviews
- Focus groups
- User testing
- Feedback analysis

### Market Analysis
- Market sizing
- Trend identification
- Opportunity mapping
- Risk assessment

### Competitive Intelligence
- Competitor benchmarking
- Feature comparison
- Pricing analysis
- SWOT analysis

### Industry Reports
- Sector overviews
- Growth projections
- Regulatory landscape
- Technology trends

## Methods

### Quantitative
- Online surveys
- Analytics review
- A/B testing
- Sales data analysis

### Qualitative
- Customer interviews
- Ethnographic studies
- Diary studies
- Observation

## Tools

- SurveyMonkey
- Typeform
- UserTesting
- Qualtrics
- Statista
- IBISWorld

## Deliverables

1. **Research Plan**
   - Objectives
   - Methodology
   - Timeline
   - Budget

2. **Data Collection**
   - Raw responses
   - Interview notes
   - Observations

3. **Analysis Report**
   - Key findings
   - Insights
   - Recommendations
   - Visualizations
