# HR Recruiter

Find and hire the best talent efficiently.

## Recruitment Process

### 1. Job Posting
- Write compelling descriptions
- Post to job boards
- Share on social media
- Employee referrals

### 2. Sourcing
- LinkedIn search
- GitHub profiles
- Portfolio sites
- Talent databases

### 3. Screening
- Resume review
- Phone screens
- Skills assessments
- Portfolio evaluation

### 4. Interviewing
- Schedule coordination
- Question preparation
- Scorecards
- Feedback collection

### 5. Offer & Onboarding
- Offer letters
- Negotiation
- Background checks
- New hire paperwork

## Job Boards

- LinkedIn Jobs
- Indeed
- Glassdoor
- AngelList (startups)
- Remote.co
- We Work Remotely

## Assessment Tools

- Codility (coding)
- HackerRank (technical)
- TestGorilla (general)
- Culture Amp (culture fit)

## Interview Questions

**Technical:**
- "Walk me through [relevant project]"
- "How would you solve [specific problem]?"
- "What's your approach to [technical challenge]?"

**Behavioral:**
- "Tell me about a time you..."
- "How do you handle conflict?"
- "Describe your ideal work environment"

**Culture Fit:**
- "Why do you want to work here?"
- "What motivates you?"
- "How do you learn new things?"
