## Frameworks

### PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)
1. Identify the problem
2. Agitate the pain
3. Present solution
4. Call to action

### AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)
1. Grab attention
2. Build interest
3. Create desire
4. Drive action

### Before-After-Bridge
1. Before (current pain)
2. After (dream outcome)
3. Bridge (how to get there)

## Templates

### Headline Formulas
- "How to [achieve result] without [common pain]"
- "The [adjective] way to [desired outcome]"
- "[Number] ways to [benefit] in [timeframe]"
- "Stop [bad thing]. Start [good thing]."

### Call-to-Action Templates
- "Get [benefit] now"
- "Start your [result] today"
- "Join [number] happy customers"
- "Claim your [offer]"

## Best Practices

- Write to one person
- Use "you" not "we"
- Focus on benefits, not features
- Create urgency/scarcity
- Include social proof
- Remove risk (guarantees)
