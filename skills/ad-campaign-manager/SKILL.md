# Ad Campaign Manager

Drive targeted traffic through paid advertising.

## Platforms

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
- Campaign objectives
- Audience targeting
- Creative testing
- Retargeting

### Google Ads
- Search campaigns
- Display network
- YouTube ads
- Shopping ads

### LinkedIn
- Sponsored content
- Message ads
- Lead gen forms
- Account targeting

### TikTok
- In-feed ads
- Spark ads
- Collection ads
- Shopping ads

## Campaign Structure

### Account → Campaign → Ad Set → Ad
1. **Campaign:** Objective, budget
2. **Ad Set:** Audience, placement, bidding
3. **Ad:** Creative, copy, CTA

## Workflows

### Campaign Launch
```
1. Define objective (awareness, traffic, conversions)
2. Research target audience
3. Create 3-5 ad variations
4. Set budget ($10-50/day test)
5. Launch and monitor
6. Optimize after 3 days
```

### A/B Testing
```
- Test one variable at a time
- Headlines
- Images/videos
- CTAs
- Audiences
- Wait for statistical significance
```
