## Design Principles

### Visual Hierarchy
- Most important = biggest/boldest
- Use contrast (size, color, weight)
- Guide the eye with spacing
- Group related elements

### Typography
- Maximum 2-3 fonts
- Readable sizes (16px+ for body)
- Sufficient line height (1.5x)
- Left-align most text

### Color Theory
- 60-30-10 rule (primary, secondary, accent)
- Consider color psychology
- Ensure accessibility (contrast ratios)
- Stay on-brand

### Layout
- Use grids
- Consistent spacing
- White space is your friend
- Alignment creates order
