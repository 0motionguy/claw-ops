# Email Marketing

Build automated email systems that convert subscribers into customers.

## Campaign Types

### Welcome Series
- Email 1: Deliver lead magnet (immediate)
- Email 2: Introduction + quick win (Day 1)
- Email 3: Value/content (Day 3)
- Email 4: Soft pitch (Day 5)

### Nurture Sequences
- Educational content
- Case studies
- Testimonials
- Industry insights

### Promotional Campaigns
- Product launches
- Sales/promotions
- Limited-time offers
- Flash sales

### Re-engagement
- Win-back campaigns
- Survey requests
- Preference updates
- Sunset sequences

## Key Metrics

- Open rate (target: 20%+)
- Click rate (target: 3%+)
- Conversion rate (target: 1%+)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep < 0.5%)

## Tools

- Mailchimp
- ConvertKit
- ActiveCampaign
- SendGrid
- Beehiiv

## Scripts

- `scripts/sequence-builder.py` - Create email flows
- `scripts/a/b-test.py` - Test subject lines
- `scripts/list-cleaner.py` - Remove inactive subscribers
- `scripts/analytics-dashboard.py` - Track performance

## Best Practices

- Personalize subject lines
- Segment by behavior
- Mobile-optimize
- Test before sending
- Respect unsubscribe requests
