# Analytics Reporter

Track what matters and report results automatically.

## Report Types

### Marketing Reports
- Traffic sources
- Conversion rates
- Channel performance
- ROI by campaign

### Sales Reports
- Pipeline value
- Win/loss rates
- Sales velocity
- Revenue by source

### Financial Reports
- P&L statements
- Cash flow
- Expense tracking
- Profitability analysis

### Social Media Reports
- Follower growth
- Engagement rates
- Top performing content
- Competitor comparison

## Dashboards

### Executive Dashboard
- Revenue
- New customers
- Churn rate
- Key metrics

### Marketing Dashboard
- Traffic
- Leads
- Conversions
- Cost per acquisition

### Operations Dashboard
- Active projects
- Team capacity
- Deadlines
- Blockers
